Easy Cheesy Lasagna

Description

This easy cheesy lasagna recipe is a simple lasagna that takes about half the effort of traditional versions.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound bulk Italian sausage
  • 1 cup sliced black olives
  • ½ cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon dried onion flakes
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 1 ½ teaspoons dried sage
  • 1 pinch dry basil
  • 2 (14 ounce) jars marinara sauce
  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 8 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 pound mozzarella cheese, shredded

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef and sausage over medium-high heat until browned; drain grease. Add olives, water, and seasonings. Simmer for 15 minutes, then stir in marinara sauce and remove from heat.
  2. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  3. Spread a layer of meat sauce in a 9x13 inch baking dish. Add dry noodles, ricotta cheese, and mozzarella. Repeat layers, ending with sauce on top. Reserve ½ cup mozzarella.
  4. Bake for 45 minutes, checking after 30 minutes. When easily pierced with a knife, sprinkle reserved mozzarella on top. Bake for 10 more minutes until melted.
